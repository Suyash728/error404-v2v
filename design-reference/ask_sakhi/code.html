I've been feeling really fatigued lately, especially during the second half of my cycle. Is that normal?
It's very common to feel more fatigued during your luteal phase (the second half of your cycle). This is often due to rising progesterone levels, which can have a natural sedative effect. Making sure you're resting, staying hydrated, and eating iron-rich foods can gently support your energy.
